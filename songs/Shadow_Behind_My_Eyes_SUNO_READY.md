# Shadow Behind My Eyes — Suno Ready (Solo Female Version)

> Developed from original raw lyrics. Preserves her core imagery, questions, and emotional arc.

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Dark ethereal indie folk, haunting female alto vocal, intimate and vulnerable, breathy close-mic delivery with slight tremor, 72 BPM, A minor, sparse piano, ambient pads, soft fingerpicked acoustic guitar, subtle sub bass, atmospheric reverb, dreamy and unsettling, hypnagogic atmosphere, cinematic, restrained emotional build, whispered outro

Structure: whispered intro → fragile verse → rising pre-chorus → ethereal chorus → deeper verse → chorus → stripped vulnerable bridge (the turn) → warm final chorus → whispered fade

Vocal: female alto, breathy, close-mic, fragile but present, slight vocal fry on low notes, intimate like talking to yourself in the dark, builds to open vulnerable tone in choruses, warm and accepting in final chorus

Production: near-silence in verses, piano and breath only, ambient swells enter pre-chorus, chorus opens with layered pads and reverb guitar, bridge strips to heartbeat and whisper, final chorus adds warmth without volume — gentle strings, not power

---

## LYRICS:

[Intro]
[Sparse piano, breath, darkness]

Don't look too long
There's a ghost inside my head

[Verse 1]
[Soft fingerpicked guitar, close-mic vocal, intimate]

I close my eyes and something moves
Shapes that float like they've got something to prove
Little dots that drift across the black
Like stars that fell and can't find their way back

I see faces I don't recognise
But they're wearing something like my eyes
Pictures forming out of nothing there
Old and heavy like they've always been here

[Pre-Chorus]
[Piano deepens, ambient pad enters, breath]

This feels so old — like a movie scroll
Playing scenes I never chose to hold
Who I was and who I'm meant to be
Flickering behind these eyes of me

[Chorus]
[Atmospheric reverb guitar, layered pads, open vocal]

Shapes behind my eyes
Are they truth or are they lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something moves behind my eyes

Am I losing hold
Or remembering what I've been told
Every night the dark begins to speak
In a voice that sounds like me

[Verse 2]
[Lower piano octave, subtle bass pulse, vocal closer]

It's time for bed — the room goes still
But the shapes don't wait for my goodwill
A dark world forming with no light
Until I feel an old focus on my mind

I see them there — do they see me
This feels familiar like a long-lost memory
How would I know if I'm going insane
Or being told a story that I've already lived again

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Strings enter distantly, building gently]

Behind my eye at the darkest of night
Trying to sleep but I'm restless inside
Is this me remembering my day
Or is it something deeper trying to say

[Chorus]
[Fuller production, still restrained, emotional]

Shapes behind my eyes
Are they truth or are they lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something moves behind my eyes

Am I losing hold
Or remembering what I've been told
Every night the dark begins to speak
In a voice that sounds like me

[Bridge — The Turn]
[Everything strips — heartbeat, piano, whisper]

(whispered) We have been here before

You didn't know but I was always close
When you shut your eyes I'm here to show
How you came to this — how far you've grown

I'm not the dark — I'm not the fear
I'm the oldest part of you still here
Let the world take over — drop the disguise
I'm the love that lives behind your eyes

[Final Chorus]
[Warmth enters — gentle strings, soft choir pad, vocal opens with acceptance]

Shapes behind my eyes
They were never only lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something holds me from the inside

I'm not losing hold
I'm remembering what I've always known
Every night the dark begins to speak
It's my soul — and it sounds like me

[Outro]
[Piano fading, breath, warmth]

(whispered) Don't look too long
(whispered) But don't look away

Because I love you
And I love our soul

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

- Key: A minor — dark but not hopeless, accommodates female alto range, piano-friendly
- Tempo: 72 BPM (slow, dreamy, hypnagogic pace — the speed of falling asleep)
- Time Signature: 4/4
- Chord Progression:
  - Verse: i – VI – III – VII (Am – F – C – G)
  - Pre-Chorus: iv – i – VII – III (Dm – Am – G – C) [tension building]
  - Chorus: VI – III – iv – i (F – C – Dm – Am) [open, searching, unresolved]
  - Bridge: IV – I – V – vi (F – C – G – Am) [shifts to relative major — warmth arriving]
  - Final Chorus: IV – V – I – vi (F – G – C – Am) [major resolution — acceptance, home]
- Vocal: Female alto (A3–D5), breathy, intimate, fragile verses → open warm final chorus
- Instruments: Sparse piano, fingerpicked acoustic guitar, ambient pads, atmospheric reverb guitar, subtle sub bass, gentle strings (bridge/final), soft choir pad (final chorus only)
- Dynamics: Whisper/breath (intro) → fragile intimacy (V1) → gently rising (pre-chorus) → open atmospheric (chorus) → closer/deeper (V2) → stripped bare (bridge) → warm embrace (final chorus) → whisper fade (outro)
- Hook Type: Title hook — "Shapes behind my eyes / Are they truth or are they lies" — question as hook
- Rhyme Scheme: Verse AABB, Pre-Chorus AABB, Chorus ABAB, Bridge Free
- Emotional Arc: Fear of the unknown → questioning sanity → tentative curiosity → the soul reveals itself → acceptance and love
