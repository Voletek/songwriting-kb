# Shadow Behind My Eyes — Suno Ready (Solo Female Version) v2

> Rewritten for stronger imagery, tighter prosody, and deeper emotional impact.
> Preserves her core lines, questions, and the turn. Sharpens everything around them.

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Dark ethereal indie folk, haunting female alto vocal, intimate and vulnerable, breathy close-mic delivery with slight tremor, 72 BPM, A minor, sparse piano, ambient pads, soft fingerpicked acoustic guitar, subtle sub bass, atmospheric reverb, dreamy and unsettling, hypnagogic atmosphere, cinematic, restrained emotional build, whispered outro, no vocal hums, no beatboxing

Structure: whispered intro → fragile verse → rising pre-chorus → ethereal open chorus → deeper verse → chorus → stripped bridge with whispered turn → warm resolved final chorus → whispered fade

Vocal: female alto, breathy, close-mic, fragile but present, slight vocal fry on low notes, intimate like talking to yourself in the dark, builds to open vulnerable warmth in final chorus, never belting — emotional power through restraint

Production: near-silence in verses — piano and breath only, ambient swells enter pre-chorus, chorus opens with layered pads and reverb guitar, bridge strips to single heartbeat and whisper, final chorus adds warmth not volume — gentle strings bloom underneath, soft choir pad final four lines only

---

## LYRICS:

[Intro]
[Sparse piano, single note, breath, darkness]

Don't look too long
There's a ghost inside my head

[Verse 1]
[Soft fingerpicked guitar, close-mic vocal, intimate]

I close my eyes and something shifts
Shapes that drift like smoke through a fist
Little lights that pulse behind the black
Like something old trying to find its way back

I see a face that wears my mouth
But the eyes belong to someone south
Of any life I remember living here
Old and patient — like it's waited years

[Pre-Chorus 1]
[Piano deepens, ambient pad enters, breath]

This feels so old — like a movie scroll
Flickering through a life I didn't choose to hold
Who I was — and who I'm meant to be
Playing on repeat behind these eyes of me

[Chorus]
[Atmospheric reverb guitar, layered pads, open vocal]

Shapes behind my eyes
Are they truth or are they lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something moves behind my eyes

Am I coming undone
Or remembering where I'm from
Every night the dark begins to speak
In a voice that sounds like me

[Verse 2]
[Lower piano octave, subtle bass pulse, vocal closer]

It's time for bed — the ceiling stares
The shapes don't knock — they're already there
A world assembling with no light
Until I feel an old focus press my mind

They see me now — do I see them
This feels like a memory wearing someone's skin
How would I know if I'm losing my grip
Or living a story the universe already scripted

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Strings enter distantly, building gently]

Behind my eye at the darkest of night
Trying to sleep but the pictures ignite
Is this just my day rewinding slow
Or something ancient that I used to know

[Chorus]
[Fuller production, still restrained, emotional lift]

Shapes behind my eyes
Are they truth or are they lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something moves behind my eyes

Am I coming undone
Or remembering where I'm from
Every night the dark begins to speak
In a voice that sounds like me

[Bridge — The Turn]
[Everything strips — heartbeat only, single piano note, whisper]

(whispered) We have been here before

You didn't know — but I was never gone
When you shut your eyes I come alive
Not to haunt you — just to show you why

I'm not the dark — I'm not the shapes you fear
I'm the quiet underneath — I've always been here
Let the disguise fall — you don't need it now
I'm the love that held you when you forgot how

[Final Chorus]
[Warmth enters — gentle strings, soft choir pad, vocal opens into acceptance]

Shapes behind my eyes
They were never only lies
Every night I shut the world outside
Something holds me from the inside

I'm not coming undone
I'm remembering where I'm from
Every night the dark begins to speak
It's my soul — and it sounds like me

[Outro]
[Piano fading, breath, warmth dissolving into silence]

(whispered) Don't look too long
(whispered) But don't look away

I love you
And I love our soul

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: A minor — dark but not hopeless, accommodates female alto, piano-friendly, relative major (C) in bridge for warmth
• Tempo: 72 BPM (slow, dreamy, hypnagogic — the speed of falling asleep)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VI – III – VII (Am – F – C – G)
  Pre-Chorus: iv – i – VII – III (Dm – Am – G – C) [tension building]
  Chorus: VI – III – iv – i (F – C – Dm – Am) [open, searching, unresolved]
  Bridge: IV – I – V – vi (F – C – G – Am) [relative major warmth — soul speaks]
  Final Chorus: IV – V – I – vi (F – G – C – Am) [major resolution — acceptance]
• Vocal: Female alto (A3–D5), breathy, intimate, fragile verses → open warmth final chorus
• Instruments: Sparse piano (single notes in verses), fingerpicked acoustic guitar, ambient pads, reverb guitar (chorus), subtle sub bass, gentle strings (bridge+), soft choir pad (final chorus last 4 lines)
• Dynamics: Single note + breath (intro) → fragile intimacy (V1) → pads rising (PC) → open atmospheric (C) → closer/darker (V2) → heartbeat only (bridge) → warm bloom (FC) → dissolving whisper (outro)
• Hook Type: Title hook — question answered in final chorus
• Rhyme Scheme: V=AABB | PC=AABB | C=ABAB | Bridge=Free→AABB | FC=ABAB (mirrors/answers)
• Emotional Arc: Unease → questioning sanity → deeper fear → soul reveals with love → acceptance and wholeness
