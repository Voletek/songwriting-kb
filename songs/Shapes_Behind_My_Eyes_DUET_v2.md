## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: D minor — dark, cinematic, dramatic tension, accommodates male baritone range
• Tempo: 78 BPM (deliberate, breathing pace, heartbeat feel)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VI – v – iv (Dm – Bb – Am – Gm) [descending = sinking, inevitable]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – VII – i – v (Gm – C – Dm – Am) [tension seeking resolution]
  Chorus: i – VI – III – VII (Dm – Bb – F – C) [open, expansive, emotionally wide]
  Bridge: i – i – iv – iv (Dm – Dm – Gm – Gm) [static, trapped, nowhere to go]
  Final Chorus: i – VI – VII – i (Dm – Bb – C – Dm) [circular, possessive, no escape]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–E4) hollowed intimate → fractured power. Female contralto (F3–C5) ethereal, uncomfortably close, whispered to sustained
• Instruments: Sparse piano (single notes), ambient electric guitar (reverb-heavy), warm bass, heartbeat percussion (kick as pulse), layered whispers (stereo), ghost harmonics, live drums (chorus only), subtle strings (final chorus)
• Dynamics: Single piano + breath (intro) → sparse confessional (V1) → bass + guitar enter (V2) → strain + texture (PC) → drums + full swell (C) → stripped to heartbeat (bridge) → overwhelming merge (FC) → dissolving whisper (outro)
• Hook Type: Title hook + closing phrase ("They won't let me go") — dual hook anchoring
• Rhyme Scheme: V=AABB (couplets), PC=Free (conversational strain), C=Free (emotional priority over rhyme), Bridge=Free (dialogue)
• Emotional Arc: Isolation and confusion → something is wrong → the presence speaks → recognition of possession → the question of identity → surrender into merger — love as possession, comfort as horror
